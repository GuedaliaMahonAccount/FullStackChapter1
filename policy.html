About TravelCo

Founded in 2005, TravelCo started as a small boutique travel agency with a mission to deliver personalized and unforgettable travel experiences. Over the years, we expanded to offer destinations across the globe, maintaining our focus on quality, attention to detail, and luxury service. Every trip we organize is a blend of comfort, adventure, culture, and relaxation.

Our team has grown to include passionate travel experts, local guides, and logistical coordinators, all dedicated to making your journey seamless and extraordinary. From carefully selecting hotels to curating unique excursions, we strive to exceed expectations in every detail.

Booking Policy

All bookings must be made through our official website or through authorized TravelCo agents. We highly recommend planning at least 14 days in advance to ensure availability of preferred accommodations and excursions. Late bookings are accepted, but availability may be limited, and special requests cannot always be guaranteed.

When placing a booking, you will receive a confirmation email containing all relevant information including your travel itinerary, contact details for our local support, and terms of service. Please review this information carefully and contact us immediately if you notice any discrepancies.

Tip: Book early to secure the best rates and take advantage of seasonal offers. TravelCo frequently provides early-bird discounts for those who plan ahead.

We also offer group booking options. For parties of 5 or more travelers, please contact our reservations team directly to organize a personalized package that meets your group's needs.

Cancellation & Refunds

TravelCo understands that plans can change unexpectedly. We have implemented a fair and transparent cancellation policy:

  • More than 30 days before departure: 90% refund of the total cost
  • 15–30 days before departure: 50% refund of the total cost
  • Less than 15 days before departure: no refund

All cancellations must be submitted in writing via email or through your TravelCo account. Certain special packages may have non-refundable clauses; these will be clearly indicated at the time of booking.

Important: Travel insurance is highly recommended to cover unforeseen cancellations or emergencies. TravelCo offers advice on trusted insurance providers for your convenience.

Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days after approval. TravelCo reserves the right to deduct any applicable bank or processing fees.

Privacy & Data Protection

TravelCo respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. We only use your data to facilitate travel arrangements, communicate regarding your booking, and offer relevant promotions.

Your data is stored securely and never shared with unauthorized third parties. We comply with all international privacy laws, including GDPR, to ensure your information is safe.

For any questions regarding data usage or to request deletion of your information, please contact our privacy team at privacy@travelco.com.

Additional Notes & Recommendations

We encourage travelers to familiarize themselves with local laws, customs, and safety advice before departure. TravelCo provides guidance on destination-specific tips, cultural norms, and recommended vaccinations when necessary.

Always check your passport validity and visa requirements for your destination. TravelCo can provide support and information regarding visa processing through our authorized partners.

For a smooth experience, we suggest arriving at the airport at least 3 hours prior to departure for international flights. Our team can assist with airport transfers and luggage handling when booked in advance.
