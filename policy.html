About SkyBridge

Founded in 2005, SkyBridge started as a small boutique travel agency with a mission to deliver personalized and unforgettable travel experiences.

Over the years, we expanded to offer destinations across the globe, maintaining our focus on quality, attention to detail, and luxury service.

Every trip we organize is a blend of comfort, adventure, culture, and relaxation.

Our team includes passionate travel experts, local guides, and coordinators, all dedicated to making your journey seamless and extraordinary. From carefully selecting hotels to curating unique excursions — we strive to exceed expectations in every detail.

Booking Policy

All bookings must be made through our official website or authorized SkyBridge agents.

We highly recommend planning at least 14 days in advance to ensure availability of your preferred accommodations and excursions.

Late bookings are accepted, but availability may be limited, and special requests cannot always be guaranteed.

When placing a booking, you will receive a confirmation email containing all relevant information — including your travel itinerary, local support contacts, and terms of service. Please review carefully and contact us immediately if you notice any discrepancies.

Tip: Book early to secure the best rates and enjoy seasonal offers. SkyBridge frequently provides early-bird discounts for those who plan ahead.

We also offer group booking options. For parties of five or more, contact our reservations team to organize a personalized package tailored to your needs.

Cancellation & Refunds

SkyBridge understands that plans can change unexpectedly. We have implemented a fair and transparent cancellation policy:

  • More than 30 days before departure — 90% refund of the total cost.
  • 15–30 days before departure — 50% refund of the total cost.
  • Less than 15 days before departure — no refund.

All cancellations must be submitted in writing via email or through your SkyBridge account. Certain packages may have non-refundable clauses, clearly indicated at booking time.

Important: Travel insurance is highly recommended to cover unforeseen cancellations or emergencies.

Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days after approval. SkyBridge reserves the right to deduct applicable processing fees.

Privacy & Data Protection

SkyBridge respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

Your data is used only to facilitate travel arrangements and is stored securely in compliance with international privacy laws including GDPR.

For inquiries or data deletion requests, contact our privacy team at privacy@SkyBridge.com.

Additional Notes & Recommendations

We encourage travelers to familiarize themselves with local laws, customs, and safety advice before departure.

Always check your passport validity and visa requirements in advance. SkyBridge can assist with visa processing.

For a smooth experience, arrive at the airport at least 3 hours prior to international flights. Our team can help arrange transfers and luggage handling when booked in advance.
