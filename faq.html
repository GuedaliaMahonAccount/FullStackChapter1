Cancellations

Can I cancel my ticket and get a refund?

It depends on your fare rules. Promotional fares are often non-refundable, while flexible fares may allow cancellations with a fee. See the Cancellations Policy.

Back to top ↑ · Read full policy

How do I request a cancellation?

Submit a request via the Contact page and choose “Cancellation” in the subject. Include your full name and itinerary number.

Name & Date Changes

Can I change the passenger name?

Some airlines allow minor name corrections; full name changes are usually treated as cancel-and-rebook. Check the Changes Policy and your airline rules.

How do I change travel dates after booking?
